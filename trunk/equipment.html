Weapons

Axe

Axes are powerful weapons used with knockout ability. Characters who can use axes and have shooting ability can also throw their axes.

Bludgeon

Clubs, hammers and maces are used with knockout ability. Flails (maces with chains) can reach two spaces.

Bow

Bows are used with shooting ability.

Crossbow

Crossbows are used with shooting ability.

Gun

Guns are used with shooting ability.

Knife

Knives and daggers are used to sneak attack with knockout ability. Characters who can use knives and daggers and have shooting ability can also throw their knives and daggers.

Polearm

Spears, lances, javelins, forks, halberds and so on are used with fighting ability and can reach two spaces. If a character can use polearms and has knockout ability, he can also use polearms with knockout attacks.

Staff

Staffs are used for magic (shooting, blasting and healing ability) as well as physical combat (wrestling ability) depending on which abilities a staff-wielding character has.

Sword

Swords are used with fighting ability.

Ammunition

If characters with bows or crossbows equip regular, bone or fire arrows, they will use that type of ammunition until it is gone. When they do not have a specific type of ammuntion equpped, they use any regular arrows available, bone arrows when the regular arrows are gone, and fire arrows when the bone arrows are also gone.

Throwing Weapons and Traps

Chakrams are thrown with shooting ability. Slings and slingshots are used with shooting ability. Whips are used with wrestling ability and reach two spaces. Anybody can throw a weapon with the throw object action. Thrown bombs have an effect like blasting ability. Thrown bolas and nets have a long range hold effect.
